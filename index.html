Visit Coimbatore

Coimbatore is a beautiful city that is nestled amidst the picturesque Western Ghats. Its beautiful tourist spots and serene environment, draw a lot of tourists, making it worth visiting. Also you can enjoy the authentic kongu foods at its best

Top three activities to do in Coimbatore

Travel by Nilgiri Mountain train

Nilgiri Mountain Railway commonly known as Toy train or Nilgiri passenger travels between Mettupalayam to Ooty daily and it covers a distance of 26 km and passes through 208 curves, 16 tunnels and 250 bridges.

A visit to Gedee Car museum

Gedee Car Museum is the only classic car museum of its kind in south India. It has an impressive collection of more than 100 vintage cars, each with a history or a unique technology.

Picnic at Aliyar Dam

The dam is situated in the lower regions of Valparai, in the Anaimalai Hills of the Western Ghats. It is around 65 kilometers from Coimbatore. .
